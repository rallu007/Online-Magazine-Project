What can you do on Social Media?



Are you tired of spending your time on social media and doing nothing useful in the most of the time? Are you curious about how to spend your time efficiently on social media?

Read the article below to find what to do on social media without wasting your time.


1. Search for events


Disco Event(source: Facebook)



The best way to keep in touch with people is going to events. Facebook is full of events that are waiting for you, so go and check them to make your social life even more interactive!



2. Follow only people you appreciate



Tudor Troanca Instagram Profile (source: Instagram)



We spend a lot of time on internet to check what others are doing. For optimizing our time spent on social media we should follow only people that we appreciate and that we have something to learn from.


When we see a post on Instagram or Facebook we should feel inspired, but we have to follow the right people for that. A wise decision would be to follow only your closed friends and some influencers, photographers or celebrities that makes you enjoy their activity on social media.



3. Show what you are proud of



Instagram Post(source: Instagram, credit: Raluca Calin



Done something great? Let people know about it! Share your work with other people so you can get feedback on it, or just for inspire them. We live in a world where social media represent a huge part of our lives, and posting your photos or videos could be a great thing for you and for others as well.


Also posting your stuff means to keep all your memories and important moments from your life in a safe place. Even if you lose your phone or you forget the password from your google account, your memories will still be there.



4. Try new ideas



Halloween makeup(source: Instagram, credit: Raluca Calin)



Have fun, but be selective. Never forget that. Not everything on social media is a good thing. Not every idea is a good one. Make sure what you are posting or who you are following represents you as a person.


Go crazy and if you are interested in something or you are good at something let people know about it. Your followers are there for a reason, but never be afraid of going out from your comfort zone, because thatâ€™s how great ideas are born.
